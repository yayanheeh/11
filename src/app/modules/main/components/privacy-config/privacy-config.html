Privacy matters! We at Cloud-Player care about privacy. We treat your data with respect and we are not selling it to any third party. Only data that is necessary to provide you a service, is stored on our server.


An "user id cookie" is stored on your computer to identify you as an existing user. This allows you to access your playlists, favourites and history from previous sessions.


We also use Google Analytics to see how our users are interacting with it. The anonymous data helps us to improve the general experience over time. You can turn that off if you do not want to be tracked, we respect that.
